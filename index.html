About Us

At Lone Star, we are a family-owned moving and cleaning company based in Texas, proudly serving customers across the United States. Founded by a dedicated and hardworking couple, we have built our reputation on trust, reliability, and exceptional service. Whether you're relocating across town or across the country, Lone Star is committed to making your move stress-free and your space spotless.

Nationwide Moving Services

We specialize in safe, efficient, and timely moves, ensuring your belongings arrive intact, no matter the distance. From Texas to anywhere in the U.S., we've got you covered.

Comprehensive Cleaning Solutions

Our professional cleaning services leave your space looking spotless, whether it's for a new move-in, a final clean-out, or regular upkeep. We pay attention to every detail.

A Family You Can Trust

As a family-owned business, we treat every customer like one of our own. With personalized service and a focus on your needs, we strive to make every experience seamless and worry-free.
